I created this game during Microsoft Build Camp in the summer of 2014 using Construct 2. The game's instructions and a video of the gameplay are below, and the Construct 2 project can be found here.

Instructions: Help Mr. Scatter, an extremely forgetful person due to a lack of organization in his brain. Assist him in remembering where his possessions are for different important occasions. Because he is always running late for each event he needs to go to, there will be a time limit when searching through his brain. To help him remember where the things he needs are, click on the required item that is prompted in the top right of the screen. There will be a bar at the bottom right of the screen to track your progress.
